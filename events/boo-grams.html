Boo Grams

October 31, 2020

Due to the pandemic, we knew it would not be safe for our members to go trick-or-treating. Also, our club needed to raise money to buy new technology for our members. That's why we decided to host a Halloween-themed fundraiser called Boo Grams. In the fundraiser, both members and non-members could choose from 3 different drinks, with or without boba, from Happy Lemon. Then, GWC would deliver the drinks right to their doorsteps, along with a free Halloween goody bag filled with candy! This was a lot safer than hosting an in-store fundraiser, but we still made sure to follow proper sanitation and social distancing procedures. The fundraiser was a huge success, and we hope to do more fundraisers like this one in the future.
