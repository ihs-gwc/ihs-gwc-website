Join IHS Girls Who Code

Who can join?

  • Must be a current student at Irvington High School in Fremont, CA
  • Despite the club's name, we welcome students of all genders!

Why should I join?

  • Learn a new programming language for free.
  • Get access to exclusive opportunities such as free field trips.
  • Create awesome projects through coding.
  • Be part of a supportive community of coders.

How to join

  • Fill out a short registration form.
  • Decide which of the languages you want to learn, and fill out the language form.
  • Join our Minga group to check in to weekly meetings.

Optional (but recommended) steps

  • Follow our social media platforms to stay up to date.
  • Connect with members of other GWC clubs on the Bay Area GWC Discord server.
