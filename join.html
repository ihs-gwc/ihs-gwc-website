Join IHS Girls Who Code

Who can join?

  • Must be a resident of Fremont, CA
  • Must be attending Irvington High School or Horner Middle School

Why should I join?

  • Learn a new programming language for free
  • Get access to exclusive opportunities such as free field trips
  • Create awesome projects through coding
  • Be part of a supportive community of coders

How to join

  • Fill out a short registration form.
  • Decide which of the languages you want to learn, and fill out the language form.
  • Join our Minga group to check in to weekly meetings.
  • Join our Discord server and our other social media platforms to stay up to date.

    Instructions to join the Discord server

    1. If you're on a mobile device, download Discord. If you're on desktop, you can either download Discord or just use the website version.
    2. Click on the invite link.
    3. Join the server. Follow Discord's instructions to create an account if you don't have one.
    4. Go to your direct message with the YAGPDB.xyz bot. If you don't see it, click on the Discord logo or message icon in the top left corner. On mobile, you might need to click the menu icon in the top left corner first.
    5. Click the link in the message and do the captcha.
    6. Follow the instructions in this announcement. They're also listed below.
      1. Go to the GWC server by clicking on the turquoise circle near the top left corner. On mobile, you might need to click the menu icon first.
      2. You should see a sidebar listing the server's channels, such as #arrivals and #announcements. Click on #set-roles.
      3. Set your roles by reacting to the messages. (Click on the correct emoji at the bottom of the messages.) You should now have access to all the Irvington High School channels.
      4. Set your nickname as your first name (all lowercase). If there's someone with the same name, include your last initial.
